Welcome to dnslow.me!

Your advertisement and threat blocking, privacy-first, encrypted DNS.

All DNS requests will be protected(filtered) with threat-intelligence feeds, Newly Registered Domain feeds(paid feed included), AD-blocking feeds, and then randomly distributed to some other DNS resolvers for enhanced privacy.

dnslow.me is an free and open source project, plesae feel free to contribute on our GitHub repository

Only DoH(DNS over HTTPS), DoT(DNS over TLS), and DoQ(DNS over Quic) protocol are provided, plain-text DNS is not supported here.

  • DoT: dnslow.me (port 853)
